The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a renowned mausoleum located in Agra, India. Commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it stands as an exquisite example of Mughal architecture and is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.
